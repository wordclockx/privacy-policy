Introduction

Thank you for choosing the Word Clock App. We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing you with a secure experience. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your privacy.

No Data Collection Policy

The Word Clock App does not collect, store, or share any personal data from its users. We have designed our application to function without the need to collect personal information.

We do not:

  • Collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or location data
  • Track your activity or usage within the app
  • Use cookies or similar tracking technologies
  • Share any data with third parties, as we don't collect any
  • Store any information on our servers about your usage of the app

Offline Functionality

Our app is designed to work completely offline. All functionality is processed locally on your device, and no information is transmitted to external servers or third parties.

Device Permissions

The Word Clock App only requests the minimum permissions required for its core functionality. We do not access any sensitive features of your device or request unnecessary permissions.

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for users of all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information from any of our users, we do not specifically collect information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. Any changes to our no-data-collection policy would be clearly communicated.
